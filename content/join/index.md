# Open Positions

🚀 **I am actively recruiting Ph.D. and master's students** interested in biostatistics, statistical learning, machine learning, spatial statistics, high-dimensional inference, and AI for biomedical and public health research.

Students in my group will develop statistical and machine learning methods for complex biomedical and public health data, with applications in environmental epidemiology, neuroscience, genomics, and precision medicine.

## Prospective Students

I welcome inquiries from motivated students with strong quantitative training in statistics, biostatistics, mathematics, computer science, data science, engineering, or related fields.

If you are interested, please email me with:

- A brief description of your research interests
- Your CV
- Your academic background and relevant coursework
- Any previous research experience
